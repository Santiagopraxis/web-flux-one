Nexa by fluX

THE SMARTEST COLLECTOR YOU HAVE EVER HAD

Nexa centralizes all your customers' recurring payments: invoices, direct debits, top-ups, and smart alerts. Fully automated, all in real-time. And when it's time to collect, Nexa doesn't send a link — it opens a conversation.

Meet Nexa →
🔔

1. WHATSAPP AGENT

98% of Mexicans have WhatsApp. Nexa collects payments there. It doesn't send reminders — it manages collections: checks balances, resolves questions, and closes the payment with zero human intervention. A collection via WhatsApp has 5 times more opens than email.

💳

2. AUTOMATIC DIRECT DEBIT

Monthly automatic billing via tokenization. Prevent service cuts and secure timely collection without any manual intervention.

⚡

3. ONE-CLICK BILL PAYMENT

Your customers see all their outstanding invoices in a single place and pay with a single tap. Without entering multiple portals, without waiting, without friction.

📊

4. BEHAVIOR VISIBILITY

Know which invoice each customer paid, when, and how. Detect delinquency patterns before they happen — with real-time dashboards connected to fluX BI.

Same system. Results for your industry

fluX One is not a generic tool. Every sector has its own payment dynamics — and fluX One knows it.

Public utilities

Digital invoicing, smart WhatsApp reminders via Nexa, 4-click payments, and automatic reconciliation. No collection call centers. No accumulated delinquency.

Financial institutions

Portfolio management with predictive BI. Delinquency detected before it happens. Nexa manages collection via WhatsApp when the system identifies risk — without team intervention.

ISPs & telecommunications

Direct synchronization with your CRM. The subscriber receives Nexa's message, pays before the cutoff date, and the system updates itself. No phone calls, no friction.
