PRODUCT: COLLECTION

Every invoice you send deserves to get paid.
What if it always did?

fluX converts invoices into real money with SPEI and CoDi—no chasing, no waiting.

What makes collection smart?

Universal Acceptance

Receive payments from any bank, wallet, or payment rail, with a single API connecting the entire financial ecosystem.

Real-time Reconciliation

Automatically match payments with invoices to know who paid instantly, without manual intervention or human error.

Predictive Behavior

Predictive models that identify payment patterns to know who pays on time, who pays late, or who will default before it happens.

WhatsApp Collection Agent

Your automated agent that sends personalized reminders, answers common invoice questions, and processes payments directly in the chat, friction-free.

PCI-DSS Tokenization

Store your customers' cards and payment credentials in ultra-secure encrypted vaults for immediate, one-click billing.

Automated Direct Debit

Schedule automated recurring collections, ensuring timely payments month after month without manual management for your team.
