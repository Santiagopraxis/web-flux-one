Business Intelligence Capabilities

1. Payment Behavior Analytics

Frequency, payment days, delinquency history, collection cycle, average ticket. Understand habits to design loyalty and collection strategies.

2. Predictive Portfolio Models

Identification of delinquency risk, late payment probability, recovery segments. Anticipate overdue accounts before they happen.

3. Intelligent User Segmentation

Classification by preferred channel, recurrence, compliance, payment volume. Personalize campaigns and improve sales effectiveness.

4. Collection Channel Optimization

Measurement of KPIs per channel: success rate, transaction costs, average time. Decide where to invest and what to optimize.

5. Commercial Geointelligence

Territorial visualization of delinquency zones, payment concentration, commercial coverage. Make decisions with geographical insights.

6. Real-Time Executive Dashboards

Daily collections, budget compliance, overdue portfolio, recovery rates, effectiveness per channel. Immediate visibility for CFOs and managers.
