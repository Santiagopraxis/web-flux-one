Real-Time Payments: The Next Giant Leap for LATAM Treasury

How modern real-time rails are reshaping B2B cash flows and why your enterprise should integrate today.

The Instant Revolution in Latin America

Across Latin America, a payment revolution is quietly unfolding. Driven by central bank initiatives, real-time settlement networks like Pix in Brazil, SPEI in Mexico, and the newly deployed Bre-B in Colombia are rapidly replacing traditional slow batch payment mechanisms. While these networks started as retail consumer solutions, their impact on corporate treasury is where the true value lies.

B2B transactions, which historically took 24 to 48 hours to clear, can now settle in less than 5 seconds, 24/7/365.

Why Traditional Rails Are Failing B2B Scale

Historically, corporate collections in the region suffered from deep operational friction:

  • Payments generated outside bank hours were delayed until the next business day.
  • Batch file processing made intraday liquidity management impossible.
  • Teams wasted valuable hours manually matching bank receipts to ERP invoices.
"By switching B2B collection to real-time rails, enterprises can slash Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) by up to 15 days."

With real-time API integrations, every transaction is confirmed instantly. Funds are immediately available for reinvestment, payroll, or paying suppliers.

How fluX One Orchestrates Real-Time Payments

fluX One acts as the unified middleware layer connecting your systems directly to the region's real-time rails. Through our API, your enterprise can:

  • Generate Dynamic Identifiers: Assign unique payment identifiers for instant, automated reconciliation.
  • Automate Disbursements: Trigger payouts immediately upon receiving collections, optimizing cash loops.
  • Unify Multi-Country Operations: Manage SPEI (Mexico), Bre-B (Colombia), and more through a single dashboard and integration.
